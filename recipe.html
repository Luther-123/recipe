RECIPE FOR MATOKE


Ingredients

  1. 4 green bananas
  2. 2 tablespoons oil
  3. 1 chopped onion
  4. 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  5. 2 cloves of garlic chopped
  6. 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  7. 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  8. 1/2 can of kidney beans
  9. 1/2 cup vegetable stock or water
  10. 1/2 teaspoon chilli flakes
  11. addition of salt to taste
  12. 1/2 cup of chopped corriander

Procedure

  1. Wash the bananas very well
  2. Remove the tops and ends
  3. Boil in hot water for 10 mins, then remove from heat
  4. Allow to cool before removing the skin
  5. Heat oil in a saucepan
  6. Add onions to the pan and stir for a few minutes until the onions become translucent
  7. Add the cumin, garlic and ginger. Stir continuously for about 1 min, then add the chopped tomatoes and the beans
  8. Pour in the stock or water with a dash of salt
  9. Add the chilli flakes and cover the pan
  10. Allow to simmer for about 10 mins
  11. Pour in the bananas
  12. Leave to simmer for additional 5 mins under low heat, then add the coriander
  13. Use a wooden spoon to slightly mash the content of the pot to the consistency you desire
  14. Remove from heat and serve
